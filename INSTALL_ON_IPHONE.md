# How to Install This App on Your iPhone 📱

This app is designed to work like a native iOS app! Here's how to add it to your iPhone's home screen:

## Installation Steps

1. **Open in Safari** 📲
   - Open this app in Safari on your iPhone (this is important - it must be Safari!)

2. **Tap the Share Button** ⬆️
   - Tap the share icon at the bottom of Safari (the square with an arrow pointing up)

3. **Add to Home Screen** ➕
   - Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
   - You can customize the name if you want
   - Tap "Add" in the top right corner

4. **Done!** ✨
   - The app will now appear on your home screen with its own icon
   - When you open it, it will feel like a native iOS app (no Safari UI!)
   - Works offline after the first load
   - All your data is saved locally on your device

## Features

✨ **iOS Native Design**
- Authentic iOS styling with SF Pro-inspired typography
- Native iOS colors (#f2f2f7 background, iOS green toggles)
- Safe area support for notched devices (iPhone X and newer)
- Haptic-like animations

💊 **Pill Tracking**
- Visual 28-pill pack (21 active + 7 placebo)
- Tap any pill to mark as taken
- Date stamps appear when pills are taken
- Progress bar shows completion

🔔 **Daily Reminders**
- Set a custom reminder time
- Native iOS-style notifications
- Toggle notifications on/off

📱 **Mobile Optimized**
- Touch-optimized buttons and interactions
- Smooth animations
- Prevents zoom on input focus
- Works in standalone mode

## Technical Details

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA) built with:
- React + TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS v4
- Motion (Framer Motion) for animations
- Local storage for data persistence

All your data stays private on your device - nothing is sent to any server!

**Note:** For the best experience, use the latest version of iOS and Safari.
